Refunds & Cancellations

Last updated: 10th November 2024

Thank you for supporting YT Remote. This policy outlines the terms for refunds and cancellations for the one-time purchase of the YT Remote extension.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are provided under specific circumstances, as outlined below:

  • Technical Issues: If the extension fails to function as intended due to a technical issue that cannot be resolved after troubleshooting, you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase.
  • Accidental Purchase: If you made the purchase accidentally, you may request a refund within 7 days of the transaction.
  • Duplicate Purchases: Refunds will be issued for duplicate payments for the same extension license.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

The following situations are not eligible for refunds:

  • Change of mind after purchase.
  • Incompatibility with your device or browser, if not listed in our compatibility requirements.
  • Failure to use the extension after purchase.
  • Purchases made through unauthorized third-party platforms or resellers.

3. Cancellation Policy

Since YT Remote operates on a one-time payment model, there is no recurring subscription or automatic billing. Cancellations are not applicable after purchase, as the product is provided immediately upon payment.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at shraey2020@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Proof of purchase (e.g., receipt or transaction ID).
  • A detailed explanation of the issue or reason for requesting the refund.
  • Any supporting documentation or screenshots, if applicable.

Refund requests will be reviewed and processed within 7 business days. Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method.
