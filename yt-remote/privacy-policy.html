Thank you for using YT Remote. This privacy policy outlines how we handle data within our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Use

YT Remote does not collect any data externally. All data used by the extension remains within the extension environment on your local device.

  • User Settings: Any preferences or settings are stored locally within the extension to improve your experience and personalize playback controls.
  • Google Account Information (Optional): With your permission, the extension may use your Google user ID and email to enable future personalization features within YT Remote. This information is used solely within the extension and is not collected or transmitted externally.

2. Permissions

YT Remote requires certain permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • Storage: Used only to save your preferences locally on your device.
  • Identity and Identity.Email: Used within the extension to retrieve your Google user ID and email address. These permissions are strictly for optional, in-app features and are not shared outside the extension.
  • Access to YouTube: The extension operates only on YouTube (https://www.youtube.com/*) to control video playback.

3. Data Security

Your information is stored locally on your device and is never collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers. YT Remote does not access or use third-party services for data processing or storage.

4. Third-Party Services

YT Remote does not integrate with any third-party services that access or process your information. All data handling is within the extension itself.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. If there are significant changes, we will update the policy within the extension. We recommend reviewing this policy from time to time.

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at [Your Contact Email].
